Craft a Brainrot Bot

Last updated: January 2026

Craft a Brainrot Bot ("the bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot designed to provide features related to the Craft a Brainrot Roblox game and Discord server utilities.

1. Data We Collect

The bot may collect and process the following data only when required for functionality:

We Collect

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Interaction data (slash command usage)

We Do Not Collect

  • Emails
  • Passwords
  • IP addresses
  • Payment or financial information
  • Personal real-world information

2. How We Use Data

Collected data is used strictly to:

  • Execute bot commands
  • Provide bot features (messages, embeds, status updates, etc.)
  • Maintain functionality and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage

  • Data is stored temporarily or persistently only when necessary
  • We do not sell, trade, or share data with third parties
  • Data is not used for advertising or tracking

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone except Discord, as required to operate the bot through Discord's API.

5. Data Removal

If you want your data removed, you may:

  • Remove the bot from your server
  • Contact us to delete any stored data

6. Children's Privacy

Craft a Brainrot Bot does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Use of Discord itself is governed by Discord's own age requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.
